Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Steen.fyi ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects personal information submitted through this website. By using this site, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information only when you voluntarily provide it, such as when you:

  • Complete the contact or booking form
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Interact with social media links on this site

Information collected may include your name, email address, phone number, company name, and any message content you submit.

We also collect standard server-side analytics data (page views, referrers, device type) for site performance purposes. This data is anonymous and not linked to individual identities.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to enquiries and booking requests
  • Send newsletter content if you have opted in
  • Improve the functionality and content of this website

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

This website uses localStorage (not traditional cookies) to remember your display preferences, specifically your light or dark mode selection. No tracking identifiers are stored, and this data never leaves your browser.

If you accept the cookie notice, a single preference value is saved to localStorage to suppress the banner on return visits. No third-party tracking or advertising cookies are used.

4. Third-Party Services

This site loads fonts from Google Fonts. Google may collect standard request data (IP address, browser information) in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

Social media links direct you to LinkedIn, Spotify, Medium, and YouTube. Each platform operates under its own privacy policy and terms of service.

5. Data Retention

Form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry. Newsletter subscribers may unsubscribe at any time by contacting us directly.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR where relevant), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for any processing based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via the contact form.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This is a static website with no server-side data storage; form submissions are routed via secure third-party services.

8. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a minor has submitted data through this site, contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised. Continued use of the site after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please use the contact form.
